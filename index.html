About

Welcome! This website has been created to house all things Harry Potter! There are plenty of images to view and videos to watch. You can find audio clips to listen to as well. There is also a list of Harry Potter related attractions and where to find them.

J.K. Rowling

Joanne Rowling (born July 31st 1965) is the creator of the 'Harry Potter' series. She lived in Edinburgh, Scotland where she struggled as a single mother. The first of the series was published in 1999 and was an immediate success launching her career as a writer. Since then the series has sold more than 450 million copies. She is very active on the social media platform Twitter and regularly engages with fans.
